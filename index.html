How it works

Our brains are information gold mines

You can't trust what people say they feel. Because they simply aren't consciously aware. Cognii reveals how consumers truly feel in their heart... and mind.

As experts in applying neurotech commercially, we enter your company as a trusted partner tasked with testing a specific hypothesis, setting up a neurolab, or anything in between. At the heart of this lies neurotesting.

Neurotesting is the art of tracking activity in specific parts of the brain using neurotech and eye-tracking. This allow us to measure cognitive load, focus, motivation, arousel, and stress (also known as neurometrics) down to a millisecond — providing millions of datapoints across different neurometrics.

Gathering and catogorizing the relevant neurodata is, however, only the first part of successful neurotesting. Understanding, analyzing, and retrieving the relevant insights from millions of data points is no easy feat and usually confined to the offices of top universities. Luckily we have decades of experience in applying neuroscience across industries.
