What we do

Our brains are information gold mines

You can't trust what people say they feel. Because they simply aren't consciously aware. We actively scan their unconscious reactions to experiences to reveal how they really feel in their heart... and mind.

In essence neurotesting is conducted using EEG headsets and eye-tracking equipment. This allow us to get a readout of participants alpha, beta, theta, and gamma brain waves coupled with the focus of their attention at any given time down to a millisecond. How we do it

Neurotesting built on rigorous scientific research

Battle-tested in Silicon Valley and spun off from Copenhagen Business School and Copenhagen University Hospital more than 10 years ago.

Cognii is the brain child of neuroscientist and inventor Dr. Thomas Zoëga Ramsøy. Taking the methodology and approach known from science and applying it to business and commercial problems, cognii has struck a nerve with companies eager to gain unparalleled insights into the consumers' minds.

But that doesn't mean we have gone all dollar-signs-in-our-eyes. We emphasize low cost, fast turnaround, full transparency, and high quality in our work. Given the nature of our work, we also hold ourselves - and the clients we choose to work with - to the highest ethical standards.

Cognii exists to advance the applied neurosciences, uncover hidden decision drivers, and save consumers and businesses a tonne of wasted time (and money!). In a nutshell, we're here to help you. Ready to talk?
