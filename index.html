What we do

Our brains are information gold mines

You can't trust what people say they feel. Because they simply aren't consciously aware. Cognii reveals how consumers truly feel in their heart... and mind.

We helped Spotify launch in India, IKEA sell electricity globally, Google develop a new VR-technology, Vodafone understand the effect of a bad cell phone connection, Facebook optimize their platform, VISA develop new products, and the list continues.

As neuroconsultants we enter your company as a trusted partner with tasks ranging from setting up a neurolab to testing a specific hypothesis. At the heart of our work lies neurotesting.

Neurotesting is the art of tracking activity in specific parts of the brain coupled with eye-tracking. This allow us to measure cognitive load, focus, motivation, arousel, and stress (also known as neurometrics) down to a millisecond. Our very first client phrased it perfectly:

Gathering the correct neurodata is, however, only the first part of succesful neurotesting. Understanding, analyzing, and retrieving the relevant insights from billions of data points is no easy feat and usually confined to the offices of top universities.
